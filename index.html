My SMASH ULTIMATE MAINS

SMASH ULTIMATE

Welcome to my Smash Ultimate Mains page! For those who don't know, Super Smash Bros is one of the greatest Nintendo games of all time. Running 1s, doubles, etc is a blast. Here are my mains:

GANONDORF

Ganondorf does an insane amount of damage and is always fun to play. He's slow as hell, but that makes it so much more satisfying when you time his hits right and time the spike right. The spike is always the most satisfying part of playing him. Also, people underestimate the amount of damage they already have very often, which makes it a fun surprise when you land a regular hit on them and it ends up launching them.

CLOUD

Okay I get it, Cloud, basic right? But if it ain't broke don't fix it! Or just stop telling Nintendo to fix it and let me main Cloud! Cloud's movements are fluid, he's fast, strong, and his combos are super entertaining. Cloud is amazing, and you should have no shame in maining him.

LITTLE MAC

I don't remember when I started maining Little Mac or if I did it as a joke, and then he grew on me. His biggest weakness is that he can't cover horizontally. He can spin up, but then you'll start falling down. So to beat Little Mac, you just have to throw him off the edge and defend it. Regardless, beating someone with Little Mac is so satisfying since he's often looked as the worse character of smash and the embarassment you can churn is amazing.
